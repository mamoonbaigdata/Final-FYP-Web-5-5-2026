Impact of Summer Bather Load on Total Dissolved Solids (TDS)

During the Summer, the daily human bather load skyrockets. This immense introduction of sweat, oils, and bacteria causes Total Dissolved Solids (TDS) to cross toxic thresholds rapidly. In a manually monitored pool, water becomes unsalvageable and requires nearly 5 complete drain-and-refill events. Conversely, the Aqua Intel System mitigates this via continuous proactive adjustments, needing only 1 partial water swap during peak season.
