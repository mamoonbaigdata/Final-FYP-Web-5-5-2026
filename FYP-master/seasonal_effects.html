❄️ Winter & Cold Weather

Cold temperatures cause chemical reactions to slow down. Water density increases, and chlorine degradation hits its lowest point (negligible demand). Algae growth is naturally suppressed by near-freezing temperatures.

🌸 Spring & Autumn Shifts

Transitional seasons introduce heavy pollen, debris drops, and erratic rainfall. pH instability spikes repeatedly as acid rain and organic matter (leaves/dust) dilute the water composition, confusing traditional maintenance regimens.

☀️ Summer UV & Heat Spike

Intense UV radiation directly strikes the uncovered pool, breaking down hypochlorite ions instantly. Water warms globally, serving as an incubator for bacteria. Chlorine demand skyrockets by over 300% compared to winter months.
